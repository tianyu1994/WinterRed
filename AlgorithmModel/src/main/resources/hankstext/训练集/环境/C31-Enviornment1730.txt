澳门塔石球场小区大气挥发性有机物研究
陈才　 张干　 王新明　 闵育顺　 盛国英　 傅家谟　 王志石　 
摘　要：研究了1998年11月澳门塔石球场小区大气发挥性有机物(VOCs)的总体情况及空间分布。结果表明澳门大气VOCs的主要成分为：甲苯、乙苯和二甲苯(BTEX)，其浓度总量平均值为57.2μg/m3，与世界其他城市相比处于中等偏高。结果还说明地理环境和气象因素对大气VOCs浓度影响很大。
关键词：澳门塔石球场小区； 大气； 挥发性有机物

Study of Volatile Organic Compounds in Ambient Air in Hockey Field District, Macao
CHEN Cai（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
ZHANG Gan（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
ZHANG Gan（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
MIN Yu-shun（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
SHENG Guo-ying（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
FU Jia-mo（State Key Laboratory of Organic Geochemistry, Guangzhou Institute of Geochemistry, CAS,Guangzhou　510640, China）　
WANG Zhishi（School of Science and Technology, Macao University, Macao, China）　

Abstract：The composition and concentrations of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in ambient air in Hockey Field District, Macao have been studied and reported in this paper. The main VOCs are BTEX, and the average concentration of total BTEX is 57.2 μg/m3,which is higher than those in most other cities in the world. The study of VOCs in urban air in Hockey Field District indicates that the concentrations of VOCs in urban air depend on topography and climate conditions of the sampling point.
Keywords：Hockey Field District of Macao; ambient air; volatile organic compound (VOCs)
